About Us

Robotics and Circuits is a student team at Manipal Institute of Technology founded by Anuj Mangla in the year 2010. We are dedicated to do projects in the fields of robotics, automation, computer vision, embedded systems, and other allied fields. Our interdisciplinary team shares a common goal of pushing the technological envelope further and enhancing the lives of the people around us. We strive to imagine, innovate and bring ideas to life. We also participate in various competitions across the country.
